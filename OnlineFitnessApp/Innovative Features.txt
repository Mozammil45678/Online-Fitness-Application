1.Basic Personalized Fitness Goals

Allow users to set fitness goals like "Lose 5 kg in 3 months" or "Run 2 km daily" and track progress with visual indicators (e.g., progress bars or percentage completion).

2.Daily Activity Log

Provide a section where users can log their daily workouts, steps, or water intake manually.

3.BMI Calculator

Integrate a feature where users can calculate their Body Mass Index (BMI) by entering their weight and height.

4.Water Intake Tracker

Let users log their water intake and show a simple tracker (e.g., 8 glasses/day).

5.Simple Workout Suggestions

Provide a list of predefined workout routines for different goals (e.g., weight loss, muscle building).
Example: “Do 10 push-ups, 15 squats, and a 1-minute plank.”
